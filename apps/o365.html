    # Microsoft Office 365

    Connect to Microsoft Office 365 to automatically bring your organization's email addresses into Badrap for monitoring.

    # Install the App

    1. Open the Office365 app page

    1. Click add new account

    1. Log in, review the requested permissions and click accept

    1. You're done. Now open My Assets page and find your organization's emails under "Microsoft Office 365" category.
